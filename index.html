Digitize any agreement as finite state machine

A Live Contract is an agreement that is formalized as Finite State Machine ("FSM"). We believe this method is superior to formalizing an agreement in a natural language (paper contracts) or in a programming language (smart contracts). An FSM may be represented as a simple flowchart. This makes it easily understandable by both humans and computers. Using Live Contracts is great for organizations and governments, as it allows automate procedures, simplify communication and reduce costs. It's also great for consumers and citizens as it makes them self-reliant, rather than having to choose between relying on lawyers or blindly trusting organizations and governments.
